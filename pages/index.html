About Me

Hello and welcome to my homepage. I am an advanced PhD candidate in the Decision Sciences area at Indian Institute of Management Bangalore. Before joining IIMB, I worked as Senior Data Analyst at Indxx, LLC. I graduated with a Master's degree in Mathematics from Indian Institute Of Technology Delhi and a Bachelor's degree in computer engineering from Indian Institute of Information Technology, Design and Manufacturing, Chennai.

I am working in application of spatial and spatio-temporal models to analyze the house price dynamics under the supervision of Prof. Soudeep Deb from Indian Institute of Management Bangalore. My primary field of work is related to bayesian statistics, time series data, spatial, spatio-temporal modeling. I am also interested in sports analytics, especially problems related to cricket and tennis.

At IIM Bangalore, I have taught the preparatory courses of MBA and PhD programme related to quantitative techniques and R programming, and was the instructor of the Probability and Statistics course for the Pre-doctoral programme. I am also working as a research consultant with the Real Estate Research Initiative (IIMB-RERI).

Besides my academic pursuits, I enjoy playing badminton, watching Indian cinema and sports.
