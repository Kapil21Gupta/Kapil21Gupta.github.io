About

I am a visiting assistant professor in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Connecticut. My research interests are in differential and difference equations, primarily in boundary value problems and eigenvalue problems. Prior to joining the University of Connecticut, I was a visiting assistant professor at the University of Maine. I completed my Ph.D in Mathematics under Prof. Qingkai Kong from Northern Illinois University in 2017.
