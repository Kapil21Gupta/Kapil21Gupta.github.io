About

Welcome! Thank you for stoping by. I am a visiting assistant professor in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Connecticut. Prior to joining the University of Connecticut, I was a visiting assistant professor at the University of Maine. I completed my Ph.D in Mathematics under Prof. Qingkai Kong from Northern Illinois University in 2017. My research interests are in differential and difference equations, primarily in boundary value problems and eigenvalue problems.
