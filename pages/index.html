About Me

Hello and welcome to my homepage. I am a doctoral candidate in the Department of Decision Sciences at Indian Institute of Management Bangalore. Prior to this, I was a visiting assistant professor at the University of Maine. I completed my Ph.D in Mathematics under the supervision of Prof. Qingkai Kong from Northern Illinois University in 2017.

This year I am in the job hunt. I am looking for a tenure-track position in mathematical sciences. I am planning to consolidate all relevant informations available via this website.


I work in differential equations, primarily in boundary value problems and eigenvalue problems. I enjoy having conversations about mathematical and functional inequalities and their applications to differential equations. Lately, I started to push my boundaries and decided to jump into nonlinear partial differential equations. I am always happy to chat about my work and interested to know about my peers work. If you are interested, please feel free to to contact me. If you are a student and interested to work with me, please send me an email with your interest and background and we can have a conversation about that.

Beside mathematics, I enjoy hiking, rock climbing, and traveling. I have traveled to 35 states within the USA and I envision being a member of the 50 state club someday. I am a gastronomer and enjoy seafood the most (living in New England helps!). Italian and Japanese are two of my favorite cuisines- for me, distance is a mere number for fine cheese and delectable sushi! Other than the food, I am passionate about coffee, perfume, and single malt scotch. Cheers to that!
