About Me

Hello and Welcome to My Homepage!

I am currently an Assistant Professor in the Quantitative Methods and Operations Management area at Indian Institute of Management Kozhikode . My current work focuses on Bayesian spatial and spatio-temporal modeling, with applications in various real-world problems. Before joining IIMK, I have worked as Postdoctoral Research Fellow with Cheng Li in the Department of Statistics and Data Science at the National University of Singapore. I obtained my Ph.D. from the Decision Sciences area at the Indian Institute of Management Bangalore. My Ph.D. advisor is Soudeep Deb Before joining IIM Bangalore, I worked as a Senior Data Analyst at Indxx, LLC. I hold a Master's degree in Mathematics from the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi, and a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering from the Indian Institute of Information Technology, Design and Manufacturing, Chennai.

My primary field of work is related to bayesian statistics, time series data, spatial, spatio-temporal modeling. I am also interested in sports analytics, especially problems related to cricket and tennis. At IIM Bangalore, I taught preparatory courses for MBA and PhD programs on quantitative techniques and R programming. I also served as the instructor for the Probability and Statistics course in the Pre-doctoral Programme. Additionally, I worked as a research consultant with the Real Estate Research Initiative (IIMB-RERI), contributing to impactful projects in real estate research.

Beyond academia, I enjoy playing badminton and tennis, watching Indian cinema, and following sports, especially cricket and tennis.
