About

Sougata Dhar is currently a visiting assistant professor in the Department of Mathematics at University of Connecticut. He received his Ph.D in Mathematics in 2017 from Northern Illinois University under the guidance of Prof. Qingkai Kong. Sougata is an applied mathematician and his major field of interests are differential equations and mathematical physics.
