About

Sougata Dhar is currently a visiting assistant professor of mathematics in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Connecticut. He graduated with my Ph.D in June, 2017 from Northern Illinois University under the guidance of Prof. Qingkai Kong. Sougata is an applied mathematician and his major field of interests are differential equations and mathematical physics.
