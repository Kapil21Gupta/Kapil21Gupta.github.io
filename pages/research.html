Overview

My major research interests are in differential equations. Specifically, I am interested in boundary value problems, eigenvalue problems, oscillation problems and mathematical inequalities. Broadly speaking, the following are the key components of my research.
  • Uniqueness and existence of solutions of boundary value problems
  • Eigenvalue and oscillation problems
  • Mathematical inequalities
